Operating the Poem Teller

Use Tweak to close or open this control panel. (Try it.)

Transcribe your poem in Text. Tell to tell it (whatever it takes).

Verse lines are inferred from line breaks in the input text box.

Indents on lines are inferred from leading white space.

When being told, a poem will pause at certain punctuation points, adjusted below. Intervals are measured at approximately 360ms, so to pause for (about) a second, use 3.
